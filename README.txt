*** Diddy's and Chimpy's Jungle Quest ***

This is a Platform video game developed in Java, using Bracken's Framework for Platform video games.
The game consists in collect the correct amount of bananas in each stage, based in an aritmethic operation, that was assigned to the player at the beggining of the level.
Once the player finishes all the stages, he has successfully finished the game.
